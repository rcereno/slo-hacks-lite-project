AssessMental

"Close to 800,000 people die due to suicide every year. Suicide is the second leading cause of death in 15-29-year-olds."

Cited from the World Health Organization in early 2020, this quote represents the deadly dangers of how poor mental health can affect individuals all around the world. Especially during these times with COVID-19, many people are prone to these detrimental influences. Social distancing, isolation, and the stress of the pandemic have the ability to let anxiety and depression emerge, which are the two most common mental illnesses in the nation.

Passionate about stopping these effects, we, a team of four students from California Polytechnic State University at San Luis Obispo, seek to provide a space of comfort to anyone struggling with their mental state.

What does AssessMental do?

AssessMental provides countless resources that attempt to ease the state of mind, such as calming audios, self-care videos, and tips for combatting negative thoughts. For individuals who aim to support their loved ones mentally, AssessMental presents plans of action to achieve this goal. Therapy talks and meditation videos are also available for a stress/anxiety reducer. And in case of a serious emergency, please refer to the crisis hotline list.
